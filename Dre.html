Appearance

Dre Carter is a 16 year old 5’11 boy with an average build. He has dark skin and shoulder length dark brown hair. He also has dark brown eyes and a faint blush on his cheeks.

His magical boy outfit consists of a white top underneath a light yellow jacket with white stripes down the front and ruffled sleeves, as well as light yellow puffy shorts with

white trim, tall white boots with wings on the ankle, and a light yellow bow around his neck. His casual wear usually consists of a white hoodie, blue jeans, and red sneakers.

Personality

Dre is mostly a reserved, easygoing, and polite person. He isn't fond of much attention or conflict.

He is very caring and tries to help people whenever he can. Sometimes, he can get quite defensive over those he cares about, not afraid to escalate things in defense

of his loved ones, in spite of his distaste for conflicts.

Background

Dre Carter was born to Marissa Carter and her husband. When Dre was around four years old, his father disappeared from his life, leaving Marissa to raise him on her own for several years. As a result, Dre took on many responsibilities from a young age, often feeling the need to “man up” as soon as he was old enough.

Dre met Ace in third grade, marking the beginning of an important friendship that would continue into his teenage years. When Dre was twelve, his younger sister Sylvia was born. Although his parents had already divorced by this point, Dre’s father later passed away when Dre was thirteen, permanently closing that chapter of his life.

When Dre was 14 years old, he met Avery, a classmate of his. They became boyfriend and girlfriend a bit after becoming friends, and they were quite close, until Dre started to notice Avery becoming distant when they were around 15 years old. Shortly after he turned 16, they broke up, due to him learning that Avery was being unfaithful to him.

Dre became a magical boy one afternoon when he was taking a stroll and saw an alleyway. After a bit of exploring, he found a dirty and unconscious Tanpopo in the corner of the alley. He was going to pick it up, to take it home and clean it as a gift for Sylvia. But then Tanpopo woke up and chastised Dre for taking things that were not his. After which he was inducted into the Tanpopo Alliance as a magical boy.
